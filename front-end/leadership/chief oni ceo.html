Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Chief Wilsons Oni Agbobu

Chief Wilsons Oni Agbobu is the visionary Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Lawson Grand Hotel. Through dedication, innovation and an unwavering passion for hospitality, he has transformed Lawson Grand Hotel into one of Delta State's leading luxury destinations, renowned for exceptional guest experiences and world-class service.

Beyond hospitality, Chief Wilsons Oni Agbobu is a respected businessman, philanthropist and community leader whose contributions have positively impacted countless lives. His commitment to excellence, integrity and sustainable development continues to inspire both his organization and the wider community.

In recognition of his remarkable leadership and service to humanity, he was honoured with the prestigious traditional chieftaincy title of Eka Dein Menim of Agbor Kingdom by His Royal Majesty, Dr. Benjamin Ikenchuku Keagborekuzi I, the Dein of Agbor Kingdom.

"Leadership is about serving people with integrity, vision and excellence."
